With a skilled talent network and no-cost training assistance, MassAbility helps businesses hire qualified people with disabilities.

## MassAbility is your partner in hiring.

[![](https://www.mass.gov/files/styles/1670x/public/2025-07/02_SecondaryFeature.jpg?itok=xsn0Did0)\\
\\
**Tap into an untapped talent network**\\
\\
MassAbility connects you with pre-screened, skilled talent you won't find on other hiring platforms. Plus, 90% of MassAbility hires are retained after 6 months.](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/become-an-employer-partner)

[![](https://www.mass.gov/files/styles/300_max_width/public/2025-07/03_SecondaryFeature.png.png?itok=ADziRq8o)\\
\\
**On-the-Job Training (OJT)**\\
\\
![](https://www.mass.gov/files/styles/300_max_width/public/2025-07/03_SecondaryFeature.png.png?itok=ADziRq8o)\\
\\
On-the-Job Training reimburses you for training new employees hired through MassAbility. After the training period, you decide whether the employee is the right fit. If you retain the employee, you have a talented, hard-working team member ready to contribute to your business’s success.](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/on-the-job-training)

[![](https://www.mass.gov/files/styles/300_max_width/public/2025-07/04_SecondaryFeature.png.png?itok=pBGZcmaQ)\\
\\
**Employer resources**\\
\\
![](https://www.mass.gov/files/styles/300_max_width/public/2025-07/04_SecondaryFeature.png.png?itok=pBGZcmaQ)\\
\\
MassAbility supports employers. We stay with you after hiring to ensure you and your new hire have everything you need to be successful. Plus, as an employer partner, you’ll have opportunities to connect and collaborate with other businesses through Employer Advisory Boards and hiring events.](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/disability-resources-for-employers)
