# MassAbility Services

We expand what’s possible for individuals with disabilities. Our services cover home and community life, careers and training, assistive technology, and your benefits and rights. Together we break down barriers to help you live the life you want to lead.

Log In to...


- [Apply for Services](https://massmrc.hylandcloud.com/customcode/mrconemrc)

Home and Community Life services offer you the training, technology, and support to live independently and engage in your community.

[Learn more](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massability-home-and-community-life-services)

Career and Training services enhance your working life by connecting you with public colleges and universities, professional training programs, and potential employers.

[Learn more](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massability-careers-and-training-services)

Our Assistive Technology (AT) services provide you with the technology and devices you need to participate in all of life’s activities.

[Learn more](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massability-assistive-technology-services)

Our Benefits and Rights services help you navigate a complex system of laws to enjoy all the benefits you deserve, resolve service disputes, and get full access to your rights as a Massachusetts resident.

[Learn more](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massability-disability-benefits-and-rights-services)
