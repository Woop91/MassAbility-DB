## Details

### Overview

The Salem Office serves the following communities: Beverly, Danvers, Essex, Gloucester, Hamilton, Ipswich, Lynn, Lynnfield, Magnolia, Manchester-by-the-Sea, Marblehead, Middleton, Nahant, Peabody, Rockport, Salem, Saugus, Swampscott, Topsfield and Wenham.

### Parking

### Services

To Make a Referral

Any individual with a disability can ask about getting services. Friends, relatives, and healthcare providers can also refer an individual to MassAbility. Our services are designed to assist individuals with disabilities in making informed choices about work. We have two easy ways you can start this process.

- **Call** (978)745-8085 to complete a short telephone inquiry.
- **Walk into our office** between the hours 9:00 am and 4:30 pm (Monday through Friday) and a member of our support staff will assist you.

Special Features

- Job Club - a group for MassAbility customers looking for work
- Career Decision Making Workshop

### Accessibility
