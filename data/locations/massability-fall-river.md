## Details

### Overview

The Fall River Office serves the following: Assonet, Fall River, East Freetown, Freetown, Lakeville, Seekonk, Somerset, Swansea, and Westport.

This is a new location as of January 22, 2018.

### Services

### **To Make a Referral**

Any individual with a disability can ask about getting services. Friends, relatives and health care providers can also refer an individual to MassAbility. Our services are designed to assist individuals with disabilities to make informed choices about work. You can start this process in two ways:

- Call (508)-678-9041 weekdays between the hours of 9:00 am and 4:45 pm and ask for the Counselor On Duty. The counselor will complete a short inquiry form with information you provide.
- Walk in to our office between the hours 9:00 am and 4:00 pm weekdays and ask for the Counselor On Duty. The counselor will assist you in completing the inquiry form.
  - Family members and service providers may also refer you for services by either of these methods.

### **Additional Services**

- Job Groups and Workshops: The Fall River Area Office regularly provides a variety of workshops for job seekers.
- Social Security Benefits Counseling: Meet with a MassAbility Benefits Counselor who will explain what happens to your SSI/SSDI benefits when you return to work.
- Services to Deaf, Hard of Hearing, and Late-Deafened Persons: Persons who are deaf, hard of hearing, or late-deafened may work with a specialized counselor knowledgeable of deaf culture and the specialized needs of the community.
- Services to the Portuguese, Spanish, French, and Khmer-Speaking Communities: The Fall River counseling staff includes individuals who speak Portuguese, Spanish, French, and Khmer languages.

### Accessibility

This office is fully accessible.
