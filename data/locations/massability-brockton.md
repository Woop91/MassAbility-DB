## Details

### Overview

The Brockton Office serves the following communities: Abington, Avon, Bridgewater, Brockton, Canton, East Bridgewater, Easton, Holbrook, Rockland, Sharon, Stoughton, West Bridgewater and Whitman.

### Parking

Our building is on the corner of East Ashland and Mulberry Street. Parking lot is in the back of the building.

**Via BAT Bus Outbound from Bus Terminal:**

Take Bus Route Number 11 to the corner of East Ashland and Mulberry Street. We are located on the North Corner of East Ashland and Mulberry Street.

**MBTA Commuter Rail-South Station/Middleborough Line-Brockton Station (the** 2nd **Brockton Stop)**

Once off the train walk towards the stairs keeping the police station on your right. Walk down the stairs (or if necessary follow the ramp around to the front of the police station) to Court Street. **Turn** right onto Montello Street (Route 28). At 2nd set of lights turn right onto East Ashland Street. Go under the bridge and take 1st left onto Mulberry Street. Our building is on the corner of East Ashland and Mulberry Street.

### Services

#### To Make a Referral

Any individual with a disability can ask about getting services. Friends, relatives, and healthcare providers can refer an individual to MassAbility. Our services are designed to assist individuals with disabilities in making informed choices about work. We have two easy ways you can start this process:

- **Call (508) 583-1530** and mention that you want to arrange a referral. A member of our staff will help you complete a short telephone inquiry. You will be invited to attend an orientation session and the process of assigning counselors will be explained.
- **Walk into our office** between the hours **8:45 am and 5:00 pm, Monday through Friday**, and a member of our staff will assist you.

### Accessibility

This office is fully accessible.
