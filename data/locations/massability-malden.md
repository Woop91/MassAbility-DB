## Details

### Overview

The Somerville/Malden Officea serves the following communities: Arlington, Belmont, Cambridge, Everett, Malden, Medford, Melrose, Somerville, Stoneham, Waltham, Wakefield, Watertown and Winchester.



### Hours

Monday - Friday:


8:45 am-5:00 pm


### Parking

Free parking near the building is available.

### Services

#### To Make a Referral

Any individual with a disability can ask about getting services. Friends, relatives, and healthcare providers can also refer an individual to MassAbility. Our services are designed to assist individuals with disabilities in making informed choices about work.

#### Special Services

Employment Resource Center

- Internet Job Search
- Resume Writing
- Interviewing Skills
- Career Decision Making

Community Partners

- Career Source

### Accessibility

There are accessible parking spaces outside the office building and two elevators available in the front of the building.

### More info

By Public Transportation: MBTA Orange Line to the Assembly Row station. Take the exit from the MBTA station that is closest to Partners Healthcare. It is a 3-4 block walk to 5 Middlesex Avenue. The 5 Middlesex Avenue building is gray and blue and has Lincoln Technical Institute and Cambridge Health Alliance located in the same building.
