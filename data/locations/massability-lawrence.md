## Details

### Overview

This Lawrence Office serves the following communities: Amesbury, Andover, Boxford, Bradford, Byfield, Georgetown, Groveland, Haverhill, Lawrence, Merrimac, Methuen, Newbury, Newburyport, North Andover, Rowley, Salisbury, and West Newbury.



### Hours

Monday - Friday:


8:45 am-5:00 pm


### Parking

- There is a lot in the rear of building off Merrimack Street (limited spaces)

- There is on-street parking on Merrimack Street (no meters, 2 hour max)


### Services

#### To Make a Referral

- Call and mention that you would like to arrange a referral. A member of our support staff will connect you to a counselor who will assist you in completing a referral.
- For more information about our services and how MassAbility can help, please contact our office at (978) 685-1731 to find out the date and time of our bimonthly informational sessions. During these informational sessions an overview of services will be given and you will have an opportunity to ask questions at that time, as well as to complete a referral form.

### Accessibility

This office is fully accessible.

For accessible access, park in the rear lot of Merrimack St. where there are accessible parking spots, use entrance B, take the elevator to the 3rd floor.
