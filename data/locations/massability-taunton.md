### Services

#### To Make a Referral

Any individual with a disability can inquire about Career Services. Friends, relatives, and healthcare providers can also refer an individual to MassAbility. Our services are designed to assist individuals with disabilities in making informed choices about work.  Applicants can apply for services in two ways:

- **Call the office directly** at (508) 823-8141 and ask to speak to the daily _Point of Contact_. They will help you complete a short telephone inquiry.
- **Walk into our office** between the hours of 8:45 am and 5:00 pm (Monday through Friday) and someone will assist you.

##### **Special Services**

**Career Assessment/Exploration Tools:** our Career Services and Business Relations Specialist can provide more details regarding a range of tools that can help you determine your career interests and aptitudes.

**Job Seeking Skills and Interview Preparation:** we have specialized staff who can help you learn how to search for a job, prepare for a job interview, and more.

**Services for Deaf and Hard of Hearing**: like all MassAbility Offices, we also provide Career Services for individuals who are Deaf or Hard of Hearing.

### Accessibility
