### Services

Any individual with a disability can ask about getting services. Friends, relatives, and healthcare providers can refer an individual to MassAbility. Our services are designed to assist individuals with disabilities in making informed choices about work.

For more information about our services and how MassAbility can help, call (978) 458-4544 and ask to speak with the Counselor of the Day. You will then be signed up for an Information Session. During this Information Session, you will learn about MassAbility services as well as to complete a referral form.

Services

- Career Counseling
- Career Assessment/Interest Inventory
- Career Resource Room
- Job Driven Training
- Resume Preparation Job Placement
- Job Placement Services
- Pre-Employment Transition Services for students aged 16-22

### Accessibility

This office is fully accessible. Handicapped Parking in front of building; elevator to second floor.
