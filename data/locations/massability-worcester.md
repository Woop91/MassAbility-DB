## Details

### Overview

This Worcester Office serves the following communities: Auburn, Boylston, Cherry Valley, Holden, Leicester, Paxton, Shrewsbury, West Boylston, and Worcester.



### Hours

Monday - Friday:


9:00 am-5:00 pm


### Parking

### Services

#### To Make a Referral

Any individual with a disability can ask about getting services. Friends, relatives, and healthcare providers can also refer an individual to MassAbility. Our services are designed to assist individuals with disabilities in making informed choices about work.

- Call us at (508) 754-1757 or TTY (508) 767-9265 and ask for the Counselor of the Day. The Counselor will help you complete a short telephone inquiry and will invite you to an orientation.
- The first step in making a referral for services is to attend MassAbility Orientation which is held every Wednesday at 10:00 am. in our conference room. An overview of services will be given and you will have an opportunity to ask questions at that time, as well as to complete a referral form.


#### Specialized Services

- Bilingual Career Services and Business Relations Specialists serving the Hispanic community.
- Career Services and Business Relations Specialists for the Deaf/Hard of Hearing fluent in American Sign Language.
- Job Readiness Workshop
- Mock Interviews
- Resume Writing
- Soft Skill Training

### Accessibility

This office is fully accessible.
