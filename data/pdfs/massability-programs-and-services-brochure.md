## Our core values guide how we support participants

# We Are

### Together with stakeholders, partners, and allies, MassAbility collaborates, problem-solves, and

# MassAbility

### innovates to bring about a better Massachusetts for everyone—one that is truly equitable,

### accessible, and inclusive.

MassAbility provides services that break down barriers and empower people with disabilities to live life on their own terms. Our programs focus on career services, home and community life, and benefits and rights. We are change agents and community builders. And we put the people we serve at the heart of everything we do.

**Vision** We envision a Commonwealth that is open to all, where everyone can seize their true potential and contribute fully to our communities and the world.

**Mission** We work together to break down barriers and make a better state possible for people with disabilities— one that is truly equitable, accessible, and inclusive.

Core Principles

People first

We define what we do, first and foremost, by the
impact it has on those we serve. Our goal is not
just to deliver services; it’s to empower people to
live life on their own terms.

We are change agents

Together with our stakeholders and partners, we
are changing life in Massachusetts for the better,
making it more equitable, accessible, and inclusive
for people with disabilities. We break down
barriers, set standards, and lead the way forward.

Community matters

We partner with people with disabilities—and what
we do benefits everyone. Inclusivity is good for
society, good for business, good for our culture.
When we break down barriers, we make life better
for all.

We cannot achieve our vision alone. That’s why
community is integral to what we do. We stand with
our partners, allies, and people with disabilities
and their families, and we measure our success in
the relationships we build and nurture. We take
every opportunity to amplify the voice of our
community.
