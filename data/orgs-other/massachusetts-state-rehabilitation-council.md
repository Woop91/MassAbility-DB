# Massachusetts State Rehabilitation Council   (MA SRC)

The State Rehabilitation Council (SRC) provides a forum for participant input resulting in recommendations and advice to MassAbility. The primary goal of the SRC is to partner with MassAbility and provide a dynamic pathway to economic self-sufficiency for people living with disabilities eligible for Career Services in accordance with the Rehabilitation Act.

The SRC partners with MassAbility to provide a dynamic pathway to economic self-sufficiency for people with disabilities eligible for [Career](https://www.mass.gov/career-programs-and-services) [Services](https://www.mass.gov/career-programs-and-services) in accordance with the [Rehabilitation Act **.**](https://www2.ed.gov/policy/speced/reg/narrative.html)

**Vision**

Ensuring that all individuals with disabilities are supported to live their best lives, through consumer-driven, meaningful, competitive, and integrated employment and sustainable careers.

**Mission**

The Massachusetts State Rehabilitation Council (SRC) is a diverse, inclusive, and equitable advisory body that is committed to promoting competitive and sustainable employment for all people with disabilities, including those marginalized by inequalities.
