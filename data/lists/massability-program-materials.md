## Programs and Services

The MassAbility Program and Services Brochure is our signature marketing piece, designed to showcase our core values and provide a high-level overview of the programs and services we offer for individuals with disabilities. At MassAbility, we are committed to accessibility, empowerment, and inclusion, ensuring that people of all abilities have the resources and support they need to live independently and actively participate in their communities. Whether we're connecting participants to employment assistance, personal growth opportunities, or connections to a supportive network, this brochure guides how MassAbility can support and uplift individuals with disabilities.

[Open PDF file, 10.16 MB, MassAbility Programs and Services Brochure](https://www.mass.gov/doc/massability-programs-and-services-brochure/download)(English, PDF 10.16 MB)

## Career Services

At MassAbility, our Career Services team supports individuals in exploring industries and career paths that align with their interests and professional goals. Whether they need personalized career counseling, vocational training, accommodation assistance, or job search support, we are dedicated to championing their success. Learn more about our programs and services in the Career Services Brochure.

[Open PDF file, 1.64 MB, Career Services Brochure](https://www.mass.gov/doc/career-services-brochure/download)(English, PDF 1.64 MB)

## Home and Community Life

MassAbility’s Home and Community Life services empower individuals to participate in their community and build the life they want on their own terms. We expand what’s possible by providing community support, technology, and training that breaks down barriers to create a Massachusetts that’s truly equitable, accessible, and inclusive. Learn more in the Home and Community Life Brochure.

[Open PDF file, 3.04 MB, Home and Community Life Brochure](https://www.mass.gov/doc/home-and-community-life-brochure/download)(English, PDF 3.04 MB)

## Pre-ETS

MassAbility’s Pre-Employment Transition Services (Pre-ETS) provide students with disabilities the opportunity to experience the world of work. We work with providers, schools, families, and youths to connect them with information and resources to make decisions about their career path and create a plan for their future. Explore the resources below to learn more.

[Open PDF file, 8.23 MB, Pre-ETS Families and Teachers Brochure](https://www.mass.gov/doc/pre-ets-families-and-teachers-brochure/download)(English, PDF 8.23 MB)

[Open PDF file, 1.06 MB, Pre-ETS Brochure](https://www.mass.gov/doc/pre-ets-brochure-0/download)(English, PDF 1.06 MB)

## Statewide Head Injury Program

MassAbility created the Statewide Head Injury Program (SHIP) to empower people living with Traumatic Brain Injury's (TBIs) to live life on their own terms. The program provides skills training, companions, residential services, and access to Brain Injury Community Centers across the Commonwealth. Learn more about eligibility and how to apply in the SHIP Brochure.

[Open PDF file, 641.69 KB, SHIP Brochure](https://www.mass.gov/doc/ship-brochure/download)(English, PDF 641.69 KB)

## Career Services for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing

At MassAbility we have services for people with all types of hearing loss—deaf, hard of hearing, late-deafened, or have a hearing or vestibular disability. Learn more in the Deaf and Hard of Hearing Brochure.

[Open PDF file, 987.26 KB, Deaf and Hard of Hearing Brochure](https://www.mass.gov/doc/deaf-and-hard-of-hearing-brochure/download)(English, PDF 987.26 KB)
