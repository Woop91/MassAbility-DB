# MassAbility Career Services

MassAbility’s Career Services \[also known as Vocational Rehabilitation (VR)\] are here to help you reach your professional goals.

With MassAbility's Career Services, you can explore different industries, identify career goals, and find a job that fits your needs. Services include counseling, guidance, skills training, placement assistance, and employer consultation. If you're ready to begin your professional journey, MassAbility is your first step.

[Learn more about Career Programs and Services](https://www.mass.gov/career-programs-and-services)

## Am I eligible for Career Services?

You are eligible for Career Services if you:

- are a Massachusetts resident,
- are eligible to work in the United States,
- have a physical or mental disability that prevents you from finding employment, _and_
- would benefit from receiving Career Services

## What services are offered for new employees?

At MassAbility, your success is our top priority. That’s why we provide support and guidance as you start your new job. These services are available for the **first 90 days** of employment, though you may be eligible for longer. Learn more by contacting your local office.

## How do I apply for Career Services?

To start your application, contact us online, by phone, or visit a MassAbility office location near you. We look forward to helping you thrive in the workplace!

## What services are offered for the blind and visually impaired?

If you’re blind or visually impaired, you can find career services and programs through the [**Massachusetts Commission for the Blind**](https://www.mass.gov/orgs/massachusetts-commission-for-the-blind).
