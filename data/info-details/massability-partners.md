## Partner with Us

With our partners, MassAbility works to expand what’s possible for people with disabilities. Together, we are change agents and community builders. And we put the people we serve at the heart of everything we do.

By partnering with MassAbility, your organization can join us in making a lasting difference by empowering individuals and strengthening communities to bring about a better Massachusetts for everyone.

### Business Community Partners

### [Career services](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massability-career-services) Career services+

With MassAbility's Career Services, individuals can explore different industries, identify career goals, and find a job that fits their needs. Services include counseling, guidance, skills training, placement assistance, and employer consultation.

[Learn Moreabout  Career services](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massability-career-services "learn more about  Career services")

### [NextGen](https://www.mass.gov/nextgen-careers)NextGen+

NextGen-ers are young adults with disabilities, 18-30, who want to change their lives. Whether they're interested in a certificate program, on-the-job training, apprenticeship, or an internship in STEM, NextGen will pair them with a team of dedicated experts in counseling, benefits, and peer support that will be behind them at every step.

[Learn Moreabout NextGen](https://www.mass.gov/nextgen-careers "learn more about NextGen")

### [Benefits counseling](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massability-disability-benefits-and-rights-services)Benefits counseling+

Through MassAbility's Disability Benefits and Rights services, individuals can navigate complex laws and expand access to key benefits. Our Disability Benefits and Rights services give individuals the support they need to make full use of their benefits. Services include benefits counseling, financial support, disability determination, and more.

[Learn Moreabout Benefits counseling](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massability-disability-benefits-and-rights-services "learn more about Benefits counseling")

### [Student and youth services](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/mrc-services-for-students-and-youth)Student and youth services+

MassAbility offers diverse support for students and youth with disabilities transitioning from high school. Career services focus on employment goals, while home and community programs develop leadership and independent living skills.

[Learn Moreabout Student and youth services](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/mrc-services-for-students-and-youth "learn more about Student and youth services")

###### For more information on becoming a partner, please contact Molly Eastman Burnham at (617) 388-8097 or molly.eastman@mass.gov.

### Providers

### [Home care](https://www.mass.gov/home-care-assistance-program-hcap)Home care+

To help participants become more independent, our Home Care Assistance Program (HCAP) pairs individuals with a dedicated case manager who works with them to coordinate a wide range of in-home services. These services are provided by a trained homemaker and include meal preparation, grocery shopping, medication pick-up, and light housekeeping.

[Learn Moreabout Home care](https://www.mass.gov/home-care-assistance-program-hcap "learn more about Home care")

### [Home modification loans](https://www.mass.gov/home-modification-loan-program-hmlp)Home modification loans+

Individuals with disabilities or those over 60 years old are eligible for our Home Modification Loan Program (HMLP), which provides no-interest loans to make homes more accessible. HMLP helps individuals achieve greater independence through home modifications.

[Learn Moreabout Home modification loans](https://www.mass.gov/home-modification-loan-program-hmlp "learn more about Home modification loans")

### [Independent living centers](https://www.mass.gov/independent-living-centers)Independent living centers+

Independent Living Centers (ILCs) are community-based non-profit organizations run by people with disabilities. At ILCs, individuals can work with peers and advocates to live more independently.

[Learn Moreabout Independent living centers](https://www.mass.gov/independent-living-centers "learn more about Independent living centers")

### [Brain injury services](https://www.mass.gov/statewide-head-injury-program-ship)Brain injury services+

If individuals have suffered a Traumatic Brain Injury (TBI), our Statewide Head Injury Program (SHIP) can play a key role in your recovery. SHIP offers a wide range of brain-injury-related services in community-based and group care settings.

[Learn Moreabout Brain injury services](https://www.mass.gov/statewide-head-injury-program-ship "learn more about Brain injury services")

### [Assistive technology](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massability-assistive-technology-services)Assistive technology+

MassAbility is here to help individuals looking for assistive technology. Through our Assistive Technology services, individuals can find communication devices, memory enhancement aids, wheelchairs, vehicle modifications, and more.

[Learn Moreabout Assistive technology](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massability-assistive-technology-services "learn more about Assistive technology")
