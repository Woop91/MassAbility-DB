# MassAbility Career Services and Business Relations

Offering a wide range of educational and career training programs, our Career Services \[also known as Vocational Rehabilitation (VR)\] prepare you for the modern workplace.

## Programs and Services

With our Careers and Business services,  you can identify career goals, find employment, and gain workplace experience. Programs include NextGen Careers, Pre-employment Transition Services, Ticket to Work, and Youth Leadership Network.

[Explore Programs and Services](https://www.mass.gov/career-programs-and-services) [MassAbility Program Materials](https://www.mass.gov/lists/massability-program-materials)

## Become an Employer Partner

### [Why partner with us?](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/become-an-employer-partner)Why partner with us?+

For employers, there are many benefits to partnering with MassAbility. By hiring MassAbility candidates, you diversify your workforce, break down barriers to employment, and create opportunities for all. This is not only good for business, it helps us create a truly accessible, equitable, and inclusive Massachusetts.

[Learn Moreabout Why partner with us?](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/become-an-employer-partner "learn more about Why partner with us?")

### [Tax Credits for Employers](https://www.mass.gov/tax-credits-for-employers-and-employees)Tax Credits for Employers+

As a MassAbility employment partner, your business may qualify for tax credits.

[Learn Moreabout Tax Credits for Employers](https://www.mass.gov/tax-credits-for-employers-and-employees "learn more about Tax Credits for Employers")

### [On-the-Job Training Funds](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/mrc-and-on-the-job-training-funds)On-the-Job Training Funds +

Designed to help your business thrive, On-the-Job Training Funds help cover the cost associated with training new employees hired through MassAbility.

[Learn Moreabout On-the-Job Training Funds](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/mrc-and-on-the-job-training-funds "learn more about On-the-Job Training Funds ")
