# Become a Community Based Services (CBS) Provider for MassAbility

Below you will find information on how to become a CBS provider for MassAbility.

Log In to...


- [Apply for Services](https://massmrc.hylandcloud.com/customcode/mrconemrc)

If you are interested in providing Home Accessibility Adaptations, Transitional Assistance, or Housing Search services for MassAbility, you must respond to the Request for Response (RFR) through COMMBUYS. Please refer to the relevant RFR below for details and required documents:

- **Home Accessibility Adaptations Provider** – See [BD-22-1071-AO-CON01-71835](https://www.commbuys.com/bso/external/bidDetail.sdo?docId=BD-22-1071-AO-CON01-71835&external=true&parentUrl=close) for the RFR and other required documents.
- **Transitional Assistance Provider** – See [BD-18-1071-AO-CON01-22804](https://www.commbuys.com/bso/external/bidDetail.sdo?docId=BD-18-1071-AO-CON01-22804&external=true&parentUrl=close) for the RFR and other required documents.
- **Housing Search Entity** – See [BD-23-1071-MFP-MF00-89001](https://www.commbuys.com/bso/external/bidDetail.sdo?docId=BD-23-1071-MFP-MF00-89001&external=true&parentUrl=close) for the RFR and other required documents.

For more information and to submit your response, visit [COMMBUYS](https://www.commbuys.com/).
