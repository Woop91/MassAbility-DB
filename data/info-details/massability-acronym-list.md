# MassAbility Acronym List

Many of our programs and services carry longer names. Below is a list of acronyms we use to shorten their titles and make our service language more accessible.

## Acronyms related to programs and services

- ### Career Services


  - ACCS: Adult Community Clinical Services
  - DETC: Disability Employment Tax Credit
  - OJT: On-the-Job Training
  - Pre-ETS: Pre-employment transition services
  - RT: Rehabilitation technology
  - SRC: State Rehabilitation Council
  - TIP: Transitional Internship Program
  - VR: Vocational rehabilitation
  - WOTC: Work Opportunity Tax Credit
  - YLF: Youth Leadership Forum
  - YLN: Youth Leadership Network
- ### Home and Community Life services


  - ACL: Administration of Community Living
  - Moving Forward Program
    - ABI-N Waiver: Acquired Brain Injury Non-Residential Waiver
    - TBI Waiver: Traumatic Brain Injury Waiver
    - MFP-CL Waiver: Moving Forward Plan Community Living Waiver
  - HCAP: Home Care Assistance Program (also called Home care)
  - HMLP: Home Modification Loan Program
  - ILCs: Independent Living Centers
  - SHIP: Statewide Head Injury Program
  - SLP: Supported Living Program
  - TAP: Transition to Adulthood Program
- ### Benefits and Rights services


  - DDS: Disability Determination Services
    - SSDI: Social Security Disability Insurance
    - SSI: Supplemental Security Income
    - HIT: Health Information Technology
    - CAL: Compassionate Allowances
    - QDD: Quick Disability Determination

## Acronyms of other relevant state agencies

- DDS: Department of Developmental Services
- DPPC: Disabled Persons Protection Commission
- DTA: Department of Transitional Assistance
- MCB: Massachusetts Commission for the Blind
- DMH: Department of Mental Health
- MCDHH: Massachusetts Commission for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing
- EOEA: Executive Office of Elder Affairs
