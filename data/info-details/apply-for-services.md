# Apply for Services

At MassAbility, applying for services is simple and easy. And our team is here to help you every step of the way.

Log In to...


- [Apply for Services](https://massmrc.hylandcloud.com/customcode/mrconemrc)

With MassAbility Connect, you can explore and apply for services online.  Dedicated staff members are available in-person, online, or over the phone to assist you in the application process and help you understand the services you qualify for.

It’s easy to get started, and we can help. From reviewing your information to assembling documentation and giving virtual consultations, MassAbility is here to support you.

[Learn how to apply](https://www.mass.gov/service-details/learn-how-to-apply) [Start your application](https://massmrc.hylandcloud.com/customcode/mrconemrc)

## What services am I eligible for?

From Home and Community Life to Career Services to Benefits and Rights, MassAbility services support your independence and self-determination. To find out which of our programs and services you are eligible for, please apply online through MassAbility Connect or call our Help Desk at 617-204-3665.

[FAQ](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/mrc-connect-faqs)

### [Career Services](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massability-career-services)Career Services+

### You may be eligible if:

- You are a  Massachusetts resident
- You are eligible to work in the United States
- Have a physical or mental disability that prevents you from finding employment
- Would benefit from receiving Career Services

[Learn Moreabout Career Services](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massability-career-services "learn more about Career Services")

### [Home Care](https://www.mass.gov/home-care-assistance-program-hcap)Home Care+

### You may be eligible if:

- You are between the ages of 18 and 59
- Have a disability that keeps you from doing one or more homemaking tasks
- Need assistance with in-home tasks to keep you out of the hospital
- Meet MassAbility and Home Care Assistance Program (HCAP) financial guidelines
- Live alone, with children under 18, or with an adult who has a disability
- Are **not** receiving other homecare services

[Learn Moreabout Home Care](https://www.mass.gov/home-care-assistance-program-hcap "learn more about Home Care")

### [Supported Living Program](https://www.mass.gov/supported-living-program)Supported Living Program+

### You may be eligible if:

- You are your own guardian
- Are able to make your own choices
- Are able to live safely in the community & manage the emotional up's & down's of independent living

[Learn Moreabout Supported Living Program](https://www.mass.gov/supported-living-program "learn more about Supported Living Program")

### [Brain Injury Services (SHIP)](https://www.mass.gov/statewide-head-injury-program-ship)Brain Injury Services (SHIP)+

### You may be eligible if:

- You are a Massachusetts resident
- Have a traumatic brain injury (TBI)
- Have trouble performing daily tasks
- Would benefit from receiving Statewide Head Injury Program (SHIP) services

[Learn Moreabout Brain Injury Services (SHIP)](https://www.mass.gov/statewide-head-injury-program-ship "learn more about Brain Injury Services (SHIP)")

### [Home Modification Loans](https://www.mass.gov/home-modification-loan-program-hmlp-0)Home Modification Loans+

### You may be eligible if:

- You are a Massachusetts resident
- Have a disability
- Have a household member or household members with a disability
- Have a household member who is over 60
- Have a documentation of need
- Meet the income-based requirements

[Learn Moreabout Home Modification Loans](https://www.mass.gov/home-modification-loan-program-hmlp-0 "learn more about Home Modification Loans")

### [Independent Living Centers](https://www.mass.gov/independent-living-centers-0)Independent Living Centers+

To learn more about eligibility requirements for Independent Living Center (ILC) programs, please call **617-204-3600.**

[Learn Moreabout Independent Living Centers](https://www.mass.gov/independent-living-centers-0 "learn more about Independent Living Centers")

### [NextGen](https://www.mass.gov/nextgen-careers)NextGen+

### You are eligible if:

- you are between the ages of 18-30,
- are a Massachusetts resident,
- have a disability, _and_
- are interested in pursuing a career

To learn more about eligibility requirements for NextGen careers, please email **nextgencareers@mass.gov.**

[Learn Moreabout NextGen](https://www.mass.gov/nextgen-careers "learn more about NextGen")

### [Assistive Technology](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massability-assistive-technology-services)Assistive Technology +

### You may be eligible if:

- You have a physical or mental disability that limits your ability to function at home and/or in the community
- You meet financial eligibility requirements

[Learn Moreabout Assistive Technology](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massability-assistive-technology-services "learn more about Assistive Technology ")

### [Benefits Counseling](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massability-disability-benefits-and-rights-services)Benefits Counseling+

### You may be eligible if:

- You receive Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI) and/or Supplemental Security Income (SSI) benefits
- Are employed or are looking for a job

[Learn Moreabout Benefits Counseling](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massability-disability-benefits-and-rights-services "learn more about Benefits Counseling")

### [Student and Youth Services](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/mrc-services-for-students-and-youth)Student and Youth Services+

### You may be eligible if:

- You are between the ages of 14 and 21

Learn more about MassAbility’s student and youth services, as well as eligibility requirements by program, by contacting your nearest [MassAbility office.](https://www.mass.gov/orgs/massability/locations?page=1&_ga=2.230152927.2138269666.1773682388-122577024.1771446915&_gl=1%2A675t22%2A_ga%2AMTIyNTc3MDI0LjE3NzE0NDY5MTU.%2A_ga_MCLPEGW7WM%2AczE3NzM3NzU1MDMkbzIwJGcxJHQxNzczNzc1NTAzJGo2MCRsMCRoMA..)

[Learn Moreabout Student and Youth Services](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/mrc-services-for-students-and-youth "learn more about Student and Youth Services")
