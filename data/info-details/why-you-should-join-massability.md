# Why You Should Join MassAbility

We work together to break down barriers and make a better state possible for people with disabilities—one that is truly equitable, accessible, and inclusive.

MassAbility provides services that break down barriers and empower people with disabilities to live life on their terms. Our programs focus on vocational rehabilitation, community living, and disability determination for federal benefit programs. We are change agents and community builders. And we put the people we serve at the heart of everything we do.

We aim to build a better state together: one that is more equitable, accessible, and inclusive.  Every one of us has a role to play to achieve our vision. We are leaders, innovators, and conveners. Join our team and make possibility a reality.

## **Career Services**

MassAbility's Career Services division prepares our job seekers to join the modern workplace. Together with public colleges and universities, professional training programs, and employers statewide, we partner with people with disabilities to expand what’s possible for their career and work life.

Our goals are not just to create opportunity but to explore many industries and sectors and provide the best fit for individuals with disabilities to flourish in their careers, giving them true independence and helping grow a diverse and inclusive economic environment.

## **Home and Community Life**

MassAbility's Home and Community Life division empowers those we work with to participate in their community and help to build a better life for them, on their own terms. We partner with individuals to expand what’s possible for adults and youth with disabilities, providing them with the support, technology, and training needed to become self-sufficient.

Programs include, independent living centers, home care assistance programs, assistive technology, support services for persons with brain injuries, and more. Each program focuses on creating opportunities for independence and creating a society that is truly equitable, accessible, and inclusive.

## **Disability Determination Services**

Our Disability Determination Services (DDS) office helps those who need benefits to build a more independent life. Funded by the federal Social Security Administration (SSA), DDS examiners work with people to determine their eligibility for social security disability insurance and income. Our services also help individuals with disabilities gain access to Medicaid and Medicare coverage.

## **Operations**

Everyone at MassAbility has a role to play in achieving our vision. Whether you’re interested in connecting people through storytelling in our Office of Communication, advocating for individual and family voice through our Office of Individual and Family Engagement, or you’re a numbers person who loves budgets and spreadsheets, we have several opportunities through our Office of Fiscal Management and Budgeting and agency operations. We have opportunities for those interested in shaping policy by joining our legal team, or who want to help staff achieve professional growth with the Learning and Development office. Our protective services office seeks to help those who are being hurt or abused. MassAbility has a place for everyone who is passionate about creating a better Massachusetts.

## **Introducing MassAbility**

### **Working for the Commonwealth of Massachusetts**

As an employee of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts you are offered a great career opportunity influencing a wide-spectrum of services to the diverse populations we serve - but it's more than a paycheck. Our teams work together to truly help change the work culture in the Commonwealth. Striving each day to provide individuals with disabilities the resources they need to start a career and live their life independently.

The State's total compensation package features an outstanding set of employee benefits which you should consider towards your overall compensation, including:

- 75% state paid medical insurance premium
- Reasonable Dental and Vision Plans
- Flexible Spending Account and Dependent Care Assistance programs
- Low cost basic and optional life insurance
- Retirement Savings: State Employees' Pension and a Deferred Compensation 457(b) plan
- 12 paid holidays per year and competitive Sick, Vacation and Personal Time
- Tuition Benefit for employee and spouse at state colleges and universities
- Professional Development and Continuing Education opportunities
- Qualified Employer for Public Service Student Loan Forgiveness Program

How to Apply

To view employment information visit [**MassCareers Job search website**](https://massanf.taleo.net/careersection/ex/jobsearch.ftl). Continue to follow MassAbility on Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn for job opportunities.
