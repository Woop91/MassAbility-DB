# MassAbility's Internship Experience

Apply for an internship at MassAbility

## Discover how you can make a difference!

Are you an advocate and change-maker? Do you believe in an inclusive workforce? Is gaining real-world experience important to you? Do you want to contribute to making Massachusetts more equitable, accessible, and inclusive?

At MassAbility, we partner with the disability community to break down barriers and empower people to live life on their own terms. Our Internship program matches you with one of our diverse offices, where you will acquire professional development skills, tools, and real-world practice to apply in your future career.

Internship availability is dependent on capacity and funding.

The next step is yours. Take the leap!
