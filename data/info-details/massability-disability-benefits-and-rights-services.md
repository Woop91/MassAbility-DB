# MassAbility Additional Supports for Disability Determination Services

MassAbility provides additional support for Disability Determination Services through Benefits Counseling, Protective Services, and more.

Use this page to learn how to access these additional supports.

## Get Support

### [Benefits Counseling](https://www.mass.gov/benefits-counseling)Benefits Counseling +

By providing you a firm understanding of how being employed impacts your ability to receive disability benefits, Benefits Counseling can help you maintain your financial wellness.

[Learn Moreabout Benefits Counseling](https://www.mass.gov/benefits-counseling "learn more about Benefits Counseling ")

### [Protective Services](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/mrc-protective-services)Protective Services+

MassAbility’s Protective Services ensures the health and safety of _all_ Massachusetts residents by investigating reports of abuse and neglect toward people with disabilities.

[Learn Moreabout Protective Services](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/mrc-protective-services "learn more about Protective Services")

## Disability Determination Services (DDS)

If your ability to work is limited because of a disability, MassAbility's Disability Determination Services (DDS) is here to make sure you get the benefits you need to build a more independent life.

[Determine Benefits](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/determine-disability-benefits)

## Having an issue with MassAbility services?

If you’re having a problem with one of our services, we encourage you to contact the MassAbility Ombudsperson. The Ombudsperson will review your complaint and work with you to resolve any service issues.

[Contact Ombudsperson](https://www.mass.gov/service-details/mrc-ombudsman)
