# MassAbility Home and Community Life Services

Home and Community Life services offer you the training, technology, and support to live independently and engage in your community.

Log In to...


- [Apply for Services](https://massmrc.hylandcloud.com/customcode/mrconemrc)

Through our Home and Community Life services, you can maximize your independence at home and in the community. Services range from Independent Living Centers to Supported Living programs and Assistive Technology.

## Help at Home

### [Home Care](https://www.mass.gov/home-care-assistance-program-hcap)Home Care+

If you’re having trouble managing your daily tasks or meeting your nutritional needs, Home Care can help. Home Care partners you with a trained homemaker who can provide light cleaning, meal prep, medication pick-up, and other home services.

[Learn Moreabout Home Care](https://www.mass.gov/home-care-assistance-program-hcap "learn more about Home Care")

### [Home Modification Loans](https://www.mass.gov/home-modification-loan-program-hmlp-0)Home Modification Loans+

Your home should be safe and accessible. Home Modification Loans provide you with the financial assistance you need to make it happen

[Learn Moreabout Home Modification Loans](https://www.mass.gov/home-modification-loan-program-hmlp-0 "learn more about Home Modification Loans")

### [Supported Living Programs](https://www.mass.gov/adult-supported-living-programs)Supported Living Programs+

Through Supported Living, you’ll work with a trained case coordinator that will coach you as you manage your day to day needs of living independently.

[Learn Moreabout Supported Living Programs](https://www.mass.gov/adult-supported-living-programs "learn more about Supported Living Programs")

## Independent Living Centers, Housing, and Transportation

### [Independent Living Centers](https://www.mass.gov/independent-living-centers-0)Independent Living Centers+

Independent Living Centers help you live life on your own terms. Here, you can partner with peers, counselors, and advocates to tackle daily challenges, build your confidence, and become truly independent.

[Learn Moreabout Independent Living Centers](https://www.mass.gov/independent-living-centers-0 "learn more about Independent Living Centers")

### [Available Accessible Housing](https://www.mass.gov/massaccess-housing-program)Available Accessible Housing+

Available Accessible Housing matches you with quality, appropriate housing in Massachusetts.

[Learn Moreabout Available Accessible Housing](https://www.mass.gov/massaccess-housing-program "learn more about Available Accessible Housing")

### [Transportation](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/mrc-transportation-resources)Transportation+

We provide you with a full range of transportation resources, from searchable databases to rideshare referrals, transit schedules, and route maps.

[Learn Moreabout Transportation](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/mrc-transportation-resources "learn more about Transportation")

## Brain Injury Services

### [Statewide Head Injury Program (SHIP)](https://www.mass.gov/statewide-head-injury-program-ship)Statewide Head Injury Program (SHIP)+

If you have a traumatic brain or head injury, our Statewide Head Injury Program (SHIP) provides you the support and resources to empower your recovery.

[Learn Moreabout Statewide Head Injury Program (SHIP)](https://www.mass.gov/statewide-head-injury-program-ship "learn more about Statewide Head Injury Program (SHIP)")

### [Moving Forward Program](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/moving-forward-program)Moving Forward Program+

If you’re a MassHealth member and require facility-based care because of a brain injury, MassAbility’s Moving Forward Program puts together the services and assistive technologies to meet your needs. This program gives Massachusetts the ability to "waive" or set aside certain Medicaid (MassHealth) regulations, facilitating the return of individuals to the community with necessary assistance.

[Learn Moreabout Moving Forward Program](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/moving-forward-program "learn more about Moving Forward Program")

### [Brain Injury Resources](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/brain-injury-resources)Brain Injury Resources+

Whether you're an individual with a brain injury, a designated caregiver, or a healthcare professional, MassAbility's catalog of brain injury information and educational resources helps you stay informed.

[Learn Moreabout Brain Injury Resources](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/brain-injury-resources "learn more about Brain Injury Resources")

## Apply or Learn More about Services

At MassAbility, we aim to connect you with the tools and resources to thrive. That’s why we created MassAbility Connect, an online portal where you can determine program eligibility and apply for services. To help guide you through the application process, MassAbility staff members are available online or by phone.

[Apply for services](https://massmrc.hylandcloud.com/customcode/mrconemrc) [MassAbility Program Materials](https://www.mass.gov/lists/massability-program-materials)

## Become a Community Based Service (CBS) Provider

### [Become a Provider for MassAbility](https://mass.gov/info-details/become-a-community-based-services-cbs-provider)Become a Provider for MassAbility+

If you want to provide Home Accessibility Adaptations, Transitional Assistance, or Housing Search services for MassAbility, you must respond to the Request for Response (RFR) through COMMBUYS.

[Learn Moreabout Become a Provider for MassAbility](https://mass.gov/info-details/become-a-community-based-services-cbs-provider "learn more about Become a Provider for MassAbility")

### [Become a Provider through UMass Chan Medical School](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/home-and-community-based-services-hcbs-waivers-abi-and-mfp-information-for-providers)Become a Provider through UMass Chan Medical School+

For all all other ABI/MFP Waiver services please reach out to  UMass Chan Medical School at [providernetwork@umassmed.edu](mailto:providernetwork@umassmed.edu).

[Learn Moreabout Become a Provider through UMass Chan Medical School](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/home-and-community-based-services-hcbs-waivers-abi-and-mfp-information-for-providers "learn more about Become a Provider through UMass Chan Medical School")
