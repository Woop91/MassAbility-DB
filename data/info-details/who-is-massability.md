# Who is MassAbility?

We expand equity and access for people with disabilities.

Log In to...


- [Apply for Services](https://massmrc.hylandcloud.com/customcode/mrconemrc)

MassAbility empowers people with disabilities to live life on their own terms.  We provide services that break down barriers, create equal opportunities, and make life better for all.

## MassAbility and Executive Order No.368

Through State Executive Order No. 368, Massachusetts State Rehabilitation Council oversees MassAbility in regards to:

- Compliance with federal law
- State laws that impact a person with disabilities’ opportunity to work
- Grant applications
- Issuing an annual public report on the status of MassAbility Career Services (the report is based on data collected from surveys given to service recipients)
- Working with following organizations to improve our services
  - Statewide Independent Living Council
  - Advisory panels created through the Individual With Disabilities Education Act
  - The Massachusetts Developmental Disabilities Council
  - Massachusetts Mental Health Planning Council
- Building partnerships with MA’s Independent Living Council and Independent Living Centers (ILCs).
