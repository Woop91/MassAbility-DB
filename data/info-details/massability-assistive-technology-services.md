# MassAbility Assistive Technology Services

Assistive Technology services is your first stop for the equipment and devices you need to live, work, and play.

## Find the right assistive technology for you

### [Assistive Technology Services](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/assistive-technology-services)Assistive Technology Services+

MassAbility's Assistive Technology Services give you access to the systems, devices, and equipment you need to live independently.

[Learn Moreabout Assistive Technology Services](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/assistive-technology-services "learn more about Assistive Technology Services")

### [What is assistive technology (AT)?](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/what-is-assistive-technology)What is assistive technology (AT)? +

Assistive technology (AT) is any equipment that helps people with disabilities expand their capabilities. AT includes motorized wheelchairs, adaptive vehicles, and communication devices.

[Learn Moreabout What is assistive technology (AT)?](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/what-is-assistive-technology "learn more about What is assistive technology (AT)? ")

### [Device Loans](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/device-loans)Device Loans+

Device Loan programs allow individuals to borrow assistive technology equipment on a short and long-term basis.

[Learn Moreabout Device Loans](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/device-loans "learn more about Device Loans")

## Find, demo, and share assistive technology

### [Available inventory and resources](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/assistive-technology-available-inventory-and-resources)Available inventory and resources+

Looking for assistive technology? Browse through our Assistive Technology Inventory to find the right equipment for you.

[Learn Moreabout Available inventory and resources](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/assistive-technology-available-inventory-and-resources "learn more about Available inventory and resources")

### [Visit a Demo and Pickup Center](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/visit-a-demo-and-pickup-center)Visit a Demo and Pickup Center +

To get familiar with the latest assistive technology, visit one of MassAbility’s Demo and Pickup Centers. Please contact a location directly to inquire about demo availability.

[Learn Moreabout Visit a Demo and Pickup Center](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/visit-a-demo-and-pickup-center "learn more about Visit a Demo and Pickup Center ")

### [Donate assistive equipment](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/donate-assistive-technology-equipment)Donate assistive equipment +

By donating an assistive technology device in working condition to MassAbility’s REquipment program, you’ll ensure people with disabilities have access to the devices they need.

[Learn Moreabout Donate assistive equipment](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/donate-assistive-technology-equipment "learn more about Donate assistive equipment ")

## Need help getting started?

MassAbility empowers you to break down barriers and live life on your own terms. If you have a question about applying for assistive technology services or need help getting started with an application, contact us online or by phone and speak with one of our staff members directly.
